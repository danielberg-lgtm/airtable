Spotlight · Dependency cascading

Move one milestone. Every stream stays in sync.

Because initiatives are linked records, not boxes on a slide, a date change in one value stream ripples through every dependent milestone in the others automatically — and the right owners are notified the moment it does.

  • Linked milestones adjust across value streams, not just within one
  • Accountable owners get automatic notifications on impact
  • No more surprises when one program slips into another

Why a relational platform

The reason this is hard today is architectural.

Slides and spreadsheets store roadmaps as disconnected pictures of data. Airtable stores them as relationships. Every initiative is linked to its milestones, dependencies, resources, delivery status, and the OKRs it serves.

That single design choice is what makes dependency cascading, OKR roll-ups, and cross-value-stream visibility native capabilities — not manual work you have to keep redoing.
